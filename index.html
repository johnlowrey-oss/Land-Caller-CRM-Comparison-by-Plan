Important Policy: The "Frozen" State

To maintain system integrity, accounts are subject to freezing protocols based on activity.

When does an account freeze?

  • Basic Clients: 31 days after lead delivery (if no new batch is purchased).
  • Enterprise Clients: If a contract ends and the Maintenance Fee is unpaid.

What happens during a Freeze?

  1. No Access: You will be unable to log in to the CRM.
  2. Data Archival: Data is not deleted immediately; it is securely archived.
  3. Reactivation: Upon payment, data is restored exactly as you left it.
